AI Semiconductor Trade Intelligence System
📌 Overview

The AI Semiconductor Trade Intelligence System is an interactive Business Intelligence solution built in Microsoft Excel to analyze global semiconductor trade, geopolitical risks, supplier concentration, technology specialization, and strategic sourcing opportunities.

The project transforms raw trade data into executive-level insights through interactive dashboards, KPI analysis, and AI-assisted business recommendations.

🎯 Project Objectives
Analyze global semiconductor trade flows
Identify leading exporting countries and suppliers
Evaluate geopolitical supply chain risks
Measure trade concentration
Analyze hardware technologies
Support strategic business decisions
